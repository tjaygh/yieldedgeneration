Our Story

YIELDED GENERATION was born out of a simple but powerful vision: to create a community where people could encounter God authentically and live lives fully surrendered to Him. What started as a small gathering of believers has grown into a vibrant, multi-generational family united by a common calling.

We believe that when individuals yield their lives to God, He does extraordinary things. Our name reflects our heart—we are not just a church; we are a movement of people committed to living with open hands before God, ready to go where He leads and love like He loves.

Today, we continue to grow, reaching our city and beyond with the life-changing message of Jesus Christ. Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking for a church home, we invite you to be part of this story.

What We Believe

Our faith is rooted in the timeless truths of Scripture.

📖 The Bible

We believe the Bible is God's inspired Word, authoritative and sufficient for faith and life.

✝️ The Trinity

We believe in one God—Father, Son, and Holy Spirit—co-equal and eternal.

🙌 Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Jesus Christ alone.

🕊️ The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit empowers believers for life, service, and spiritual growth.

💒 The Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, grow, and make disciples.

🌍 The Great Commission

We believe we are called to share the gospel locally and globally.

Our Journey

2020

Humble Beginnings

YIELDED GENERATION began as a small home fellowship with 12 people hungry for God.

2021

First Public Service

We held our first public Sunday service with 80 people in a rented hall.

2022

Community Impact

Launched our food outreach program, serving over 500 families in our community.

2024

Growing Family

We grew to over 300 members and launched our children's ministry and life groups.

Today

Looking Forward

We continue to expand our reach, dreaming of a permanent home and greater community impact.
