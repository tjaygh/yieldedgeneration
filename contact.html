Frequently Asked Questions

What time should I arrive?

We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes early, especially if it's your first visit, to find parking and get settled.

Is there children's ministry?

Yes! We have safe, engaging children's programs for ages 0-12 during our Sunday service.

What should I wear?

Come as you are! You'll see everything from casual to business attire. We care more about your heart than your outfit.

How can I get involved?

Start by attending a service, then join a Life Group or volunteer with one of our serve teams. We'd love to help you find your place!

Do you have online services?

Yes! We livestream our Sunday service at 10:00 AM on YouTube and Facebook. Catch the replay anytime.

How do I become a member?

Attend our New Members Class (held monthly) to learn about our vision and values. Contact us for the next date!
